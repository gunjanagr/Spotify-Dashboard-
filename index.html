Top Genre

Classical leads total streams globally, followed by Rock and Indie — surprising given pop music's cultural dominance.

Skip Behavior

Free and Premium users skip at nearly identical rates (~20%), suggesting skipping is driven by content preference, not platform type.

Top Markets

South Korea, Sweden, and South Africa lead total hours streamed — strong engagement outside traditional Western markets.
